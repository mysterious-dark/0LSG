Welcome to Frostlight

In the heart of a cold, silent world lies Frostlight — a sprawling urban expanse wrapped in ice and echoes. Once a thriving technological marvel, it now glimmers under a clear, unbroken sky, bathed in sunlight and frost. Snow-laced buildings stretch to the horizon, untouched by time, while the soft chill of the wind whispers stories of forgotten wars and buried secrets.

This is your home, your hub, and your gateway to the unknown. Choose your path carefully — every step into the frozen city unveils new possibilities… and new dangers.
